University of Southern California
We are announcing the launch of the IKEA Furniture Assembly environment as a first-of-its-kind benchmark for testing and accelerating the automation of physical assembly processes. The IKEA Furniture Assembly environment aims to test planning, vision, and dextrous manipulation together - a requirement that current state of the art robots still struggle with. The Furniture Assembly environment will be a stepping stone towards a new era of robots that can understand, plan, and skillfully execute precise physical manipulations, a far departure from the traditional robots of today.

This project was supported by the Center for Super Intelligence, Kakao Brain, and SKT.
